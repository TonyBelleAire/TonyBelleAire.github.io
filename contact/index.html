Account/Data Deletion

To make a request for Account/Data deletion, please email us at support@itsbelleaire.com, with the subject "Delete My Account". Upon completion of the request, your account and all data associated with your account will be deleted. Deletion of your account will include items which may have been purchased by your account and are not subject to refund. In order for the request to be completed, you must email us using the email that is associated with your account. A confirmation of deletion will be emailed upon completion.
