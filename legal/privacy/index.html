Your Rights

We want you to feel comfortable sharing your information with us, but sometimes it helps to know you can exercise certain rights over information about you. So, subject to the exceptions at the bottom of Section 3, we want to make sure you have a few options for making sure that you can exercise some control over your information. Any requests may be made to support@itsbelleaire.com. When submitting a request, please be patient as we will respond to your request as soon as possible, but processing your request may take some time.

Furthermore, we may reject requests, including if we are unable to verify your identity, if the request is unlawful or invalid, or if it infringes on the intellectual property or privacy rights of another person.

Access. At any time, you may request a copy of all information we have collected from and about you.

Rectification/Correction. If you believe some of the data we’ve collected about you is inaccurate, then please let us know. We will do our best to correct any misinformation. Please note, however, that information generated based on observation may not be corrected to your instructions if we determine that it is in fact accurate.

Right to Delete. If at any time you wish for us to delete the information we have collected about you, you need only ask. Unless we have outstanding lawful obligations preventing us from doing so, we will delete any and all information we have about you. Please note, however, that deletion of all of your information will destroy your profile and a new one will need to be set up if you wish to continue using our services.

Consent Withdrawal. At any time, you may withdraw your consent for us to collect data on you. Please note that much of the data we collect on you is used to make our services work. Should you withdraw consent to use your information, our services may not work or functionality may be reduced.
